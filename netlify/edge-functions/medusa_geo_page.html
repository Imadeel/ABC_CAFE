About this restaurant
What to expect
Medusa Osteria Romana serves classic Roman cuisine — cacio e pepe made tableside, carbonara, wood-fired supplì — in a warm dining room in Fortitude Valley. The kitchen runs à la carte every night. Natural wines from Lazio. No set menus required. Suitable for couples, business dinners, and groups up to 10.
Booking policy
Reservations held for 15 minutes past booking time. Walk-ins welcome but recommended to book on Friday and Saturday evenings. No deposit required for parties under 6. Private dining room available for groups of 10 or more.
